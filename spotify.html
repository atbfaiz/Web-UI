Login With Phone Number

As we work on finding more ways for people to start using Spotify, a few months ago, we started letting people sign up and log in with their phone numbers. India was the first country to have this, and we’re scaling this to other countries where it could be a preferred method.

The challenge was to let people in countries (like India and Brazil) sign up for services using their phone number. This wasn’t available on Spotify.

The solution was to build a method where people could use their phone number to sign up or log in to their Spotify account. Success is measured by the numbers/percentage of people using this option to sign up. 

My role on this cross-functional project was to partner with the design lead on this project right from the start. We did a lot of analysis around how other apps do this, tested qualitatively, and gathered several insights on how to structure the flow, the copy, and the tone of voice. I also worked very closely with the developers to understand several error and edge cases, and to translate them into user-facing language. 


Major shoutout to the awesome developers who very patiently took me through all the different scenarios! As of March 2020, this is an ongoing project and will probably have some changes.

Language Selection

Languages play a big role when it comes to music in India, and during research we found that people expected to choose and set multiple language preferences to search, browse, listen, and get recommendations. This is now being scaled to other countries as part of our on-boarding experience.

The challenge was to make the first step of the on-boarding process conversational.

The solution was to describe the steps in clear, actionable words. We measure success by the number/percentage of users who complete this step in the on-boarding process.

My role was to work with existing insights and the designers to make the language selection conversational, warm, and helpful. This cascades into different parts of the app, including the Settings page, where I helped make it intuitive and improve the information architecture. 


This was my first project at Spotify. I was super excited to be part of the team that was bringing Spotify home!
